We are totally against unauthorized access to other people's wallets. Before entering the company's database, each wallet is well-tested by automized software, which allows to accurately determine whether the wallet is abandoned. One of the important factors when checking a wallet is the time of last transaction. In simple words, if there were not any transactions over the past few years, this wallet will be added in our database, however, these are not all the factors that we use to check, which practically excludes the misuse of the wallet.
In case if the owner of the wallet has been founded or contacted us, we will provide access or leave this wallet without action if owner wishes so. To prove wallet’s ownership, owner must answer questions and provide certain proofs of ownership. If the proof is sufficient, we have 2 options: First is to leave the wallet without any action if owner wish so, Second option: we can search keys for this wallet and provide it to owner by signing a contract. In this case owner must pay 1-25% fee for our service depending on amount of funds on the wallet.
