About us

Arnold Mitchell

CEO of the company

The idea of creating a company appeared in 2020 when a friend of the company's founder, Arnold Mitchell, asked him to help restore access to a Bitcoin wallet. At first, Arnold refused, considering this idea a failure because it would take a very long time to find a secret key that would allow restoring access. Nevertheless, he was interested in the task, as it hurt his professional pride. He has long held a leading position in the encryption and cryptography department of Coinbase. As a result, after several months of trials and attempts, he wrote his software using a new principle of matching combinations. We used an algorithm that made it possible to compare the options during selection, discarding the false ones. This algorithm reduced the time and allowed the process a much larger number of combinations per unit. After Arnold got access to his friend's wallet was restored, Arnold thought he could scale the idea. With its servers and software written by Arnold, it was possible to access a lot of forgotten wallets. Arnold quickly found the first investors, invested part of his funds to purchase equipment, and opened the company's first office. In 2021, Arnold founded the company Leftcoins. By the end of 2021, the company had become profitable. By the end of 2021, the company became profitable, and we opened two more data centers in 2022. ...Read the entire text

Data centers

Data center in Melbourne

In 2021, one of the largest data centers opened in the Australian city of Melbourne - the 1100 square meters area houses more than 800 servers, including 100 servers of the VDM-WX1 type. Thanks to the solar panels we installed around the center, we reduced energy consumption by 40%.

Data center in Lovona

The company's second-largest data centre, which opened in 2022, is located in Lovön, near Stockholm. There are 640 servers in this center, with a total area of 840 square meters. A distinctive feature of this data center is an advanced cooling system, which made it possible to place more than 80 VDM-WX1 type servers in it

Data center in San Francisco

The company opened its third-largest data center in the suburbs of San Francisco in 2022 to expand its presence in the US and Canadian markets. The center houses 400 servers, of which 40 are VDM-X750. Soon, the company plans to open a data center in Montreal with 775 servers.

Technologies we use

We have equipped our data centers with modern servers that allow large amounts of information processing. We divide servers into Virtual Private Machine (VPM) and Virtual Dedicated Machines (VDM)

A Virtual Private Machine (VPM) is a virtual server based on cloud technologies. Each virtual server occupies 1/100 of the space on the physical server. Due to reduced productivity, we can deploy them quickly and in almost unlimited quantities.

A Virtual Dedicated Machine (VDM) is a physical server based on Intel Data Center Systems. Depending on the server configuration, these servers can process a high volume of information. We don't divide premium segment servers VDM-X750 and VDM-WX1 into virtual because of the complexity and cost of equipment. ...Read the entire text

Security and ethics

We start working with a wallet only if no one has used the wallet and no transactions have occurred over the last ten years. Suppose the wallet owner provides proof of ownership of the wallet (amount of funds, date of the previous transaction). In that case, we will return the funds to him minus a commission in the amount of 10%.
